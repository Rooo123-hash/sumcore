Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book movie tickets?

You can book movie tickets by clicking on the "Book Tickets" button under each movie on the Now Showing page.

2. Can I cancel or modify my booking?

No, once a ticket is booked, it cannot be canceled or modified. Please make sure the details are correct before booking.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards and digital wallets like Google Pay and Apple Pay.

4. Is there a refund if the movie gets canceled?

Yes, in case of cancellation from our side, you'll receive a full refund within 3-5 business days.
